Communication and distributed systems at the HAW Hamburg


The „Communication and Distributed System“ (CaDS) group is part of the Department Informatik at the Hamburg University of Applied Sciences (HAW Hamburg). The research objectives include in general networking tasks, distributed systems, and (embedded) system design with applications in wired and wireless networks.

 

Currently the group has a special interest on transport protocols like TCP and SCTP and web real-time communication protocols like WebRTC.

 

The CaDS group  is always looking for motivated students who are interested in the challenges of the future Internet.



"We don’t build the systems, we provide an infrastructure to connect and deploy them."
