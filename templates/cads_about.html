CaDS at the Hamburg University of Applied Scienes


The group communication and distributed systems (AG CaDS) faces the challenges of today’s networks.
Smart infrastructure for smart systems!

GOALS

We focus on four major goals:

  • Improve and extend current Internet technology
    • Real implmentations
    • Real setups
    • Practical solutions
  • Support dialogue for the future principles on the Internet
    • Open platform
    • Open standards
    • Free access
  • Improve student training for smart systems
    • Programming skills
    • Network and protocol diagnostic
    • Smart students for smart systems
  • Initiate cooperation to create smart infrastructure


More information about the goals: Positionspapier Digitalisierung (german)
